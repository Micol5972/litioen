Climate Action in Bolivia

Promoting CO₂ purification using lithium hydroxide for a sustainable future.

Vision and Mission

Vision

Contribute to the education of future generations through workshops that promote the sustainable use of lithium technologies, develop technical skills, encourage innovation, and create environmental awareness to lead actions against climate change.

Mission

Train new generations in the sustainable use of lithium technologies, promoting practical education, research, and environmental awareness to drive sustainable development and the energy transition.

Scholarship System

We are proud to announce a scholarship system that will reward the top three projects related to the application of lithium hydroxide in CO₂ purification. Young people from all departments of Bolivia can participate by presenting their innovative ideas. The best proposals will receive:

  • 1st Place: 100% scholarship at any university in the country and full project funding.
  • 2nd Place: 75% scholarship at the university of their choice in Bolivia.
  • 3rd Place: 50% scholarship at the university of their choice in Bolivia.

Presentations will be open to young people from all departments of the country, and only the top three ideas will be selected.
